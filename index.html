Game Overview

  • The goal of InFill is to occupy as much space as possible!
  • To advance to the next level, simply take up more space.
  • For our purposes, you prefer blues and greens. Reds and oranges? Not so much.
  • You can hinder your opponents growth by clicking on their bubbles.
  • Your bubbles will grow faster with a slight prod. But don't bother them too much!
